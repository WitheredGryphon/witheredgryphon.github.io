About This Novel

Eri is an ordinary looking freshman who attends Crobi Academy. With her parents working as researchers overseas, she is left all alone at her grandparents' house after her grandfather's death. Left by herself, Eri continues to obsess over her ball jointed dolls; a hobby she's unable to tell anyone about, even her friends.
