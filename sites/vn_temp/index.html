Gateway and Beginner Novels
Katawa Shoujo
Nakai Hisao—a normal high school student—one day collapses and is diagnosed with arrhythmia. Due to this, his parents have enrolled him in a school for impaired students.

Genres: Romance, Slice of Life
Steins;Gate
A group of friends have modified their microwave to send text messages to the past. An organization named SERN who has been doing their own research on time travel is now hunting them down.
Rating:
Genres: Suspense, Thriller, Sci-Fi
999
Nine people have been kidnapped by a mysterious person called Zero and are forced to participate in a survival game called the "Nonary Game".
Rating:
Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller
Phoenix Wright
Phoenix Wright is a rookie lawyer fresh out of law school, taking a position at Fey & Co. Law Offices run by Mia Fey, a defense attorney that helped to acquit Wright of murder several years prior.
Rating:
Genres: Mystery, Drama
Dangan Ronpa
Highly gifted individuals from across the nation have been handpicked to attend Hope's Peak Academy—a school for "ultimate" students. But when they arrive they find themselves thrust into a killing game to ensure their own survival.
Rating:
Genres: Mystery, Drama
Fate/Stay Night
Emiya Shirou—a high school student who has learned rudimentary magic—finds himself engaged in the Holy Grail War, a ritual to produce the greatest holy artifact said to be capable of granting any wish.
Rating:
Genres: Action, Fantasy
Planetarian
Humanity is nearly extinct and a perpetual and lethal Rain falls on the Earth. "Junkers" plunder from the ruins of civilization. One such Junker sneaks into the most dangerous of all ruins—a "Sarcophagus City".
Rating:
Genres: Drama
VA-11 HALL-A
You are a bartender at VA-11 HALL-A, nicknamed "Valhalla", in a dystopia where nanomachines oppress the people, and the terrifying White Knights dictate humanity. What stories will you bear witness to?
Rating:
Genres: Sci-Fi
Narcissu
You fall terminally ill shortly after your twentieth birthday and are admitted to a hospital. There you meet Setsumi, another terminally ill patient. Both of you refusing to die at home or in a hospital, you decide to run away together.
Rating:
Genres: Drama
Little Busters!
Riki was a child when his parents died, leaving him hopeless and depressed. What saved him was a group of four kids calling themselves the Little Busters.
Rating:
Genres: Comedy, Drama, Slice of Life

Dies irae
